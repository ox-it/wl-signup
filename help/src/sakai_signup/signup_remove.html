Sign-up tool:  Remove meeting(s)

The Meetings page shows all the site’s meetings in table format including title, location, date, time, and so forth.  They can be ordered with the column headings and filtered with the “View” selection box.  Recurring meetings, those created as a set with a single title, can be expanded or contracted with the checkbox.

To remove a meeting: 
Check the "Remove" checkbox alongside the meeting you wish to remove, then click the "Remove Meetings" button.

You will receive a confirmation message: "Are you sure you want to permanently remove the meeting(s)?". Select "OK" or "Cancel".

You can also remove an entire series of recurring meetings. Uncheck the box "Expand all recurring meetings". This provides the collapsed view. Check the "Remove" checkbox alongside the series you wish to remove, then click the "Remove Meetings" button.

Participants who have signed up for the meeting will receive an automatic email notification that the meeting has been cancelled. The meeting(s) will be automatically removed from the personal calendars of the selected meeting coordinators and from the personal calendars of participants who had saved the ical appointment.
